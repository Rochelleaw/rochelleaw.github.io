Research

The Aw Lab develops engineering biology technologies for sustainable biomanufacturing. Our research combines host engineering, cell-free systems and glycoengineering to create next-generation platforms for the production of biologics and other high-value biomolecules.

By integrating microbial engineering with cell-free approaches, we aim to accelerate biological design, improve manufacturing efficiency and expand the range of molecules accessible through biotechnology.

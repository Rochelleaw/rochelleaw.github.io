Pichia Engineering

The Aw Lab engineers Pichia pastoris as a platform for the production of biologics and other high-value biomolecules.

Research Overview

We develop engineering biology approaches for the design, construction and optimisation of Pichia pastoris-based production systems. Our research combines strain engineering, engineering biology and bioprocess development to improve the production of recombinant proteins and other valuable products.

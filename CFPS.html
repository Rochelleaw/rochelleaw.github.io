Cell-Free Protein Synthesis

The Aw Lab develops cell-free protein synthesis systems for rapid protein production and engineering biology applications.

Research Overview

Cell-free protein synthesis enables the production of proteins outside living cells, providing a rapid and flexible platform for biological design and testing.

Our research focuses on developing eukaryotic cell-free systems derived from Pichia pastoris, expanding their capabilities and applying them to high-throughput protein production and screening.

Research Themes

Eukaryotic cell-free platform

Developing and optimising Pichia pastoris-derived cell-free protein synthesis systems for protein production and synthetic biology applications.

High-throughput screening platform

Building high-throughput workflows for rapid antibody production, screening and characterisation using cell-free systems.

Welcome to the Aw Lab

The Aw Lab develops engineering biology platforms for next-generation biomanufacturing. We combine microbial engineering, cell-free protein synthesis and in vitro glycosylation to accelerate the production of biologics and other high-value biomolecules.

Research Areas

Pichia Engineering

Engineering Pichia pastoris as a platform for biologic production and biotechnology.

Cell-Free Protein Synthesis

Harnessing cell-free systems for rapid design, testing and protein production.

In Vitro Glycosylation

Developing cell-free glycosylation technologies for bespoke glycoprotein synthesis.

Cell-free protein synthesis

The Aw Lab engineers Pichia pastoris as a platform for the production of biologics and other high-value biomolecules.

Research Overview

We develop engineering biology approaches for the design, construction and optimisation of Pichia pastoris-based production systems.

Our research combines strain engineering, engineering biology and bioprocess development to improve the production of recombinant proteins and other valuable products.

Research Themes

Eukaryotic cell-free platform

Expanding on the Pichia pastoris cell-free protein synthesis system.

High-through screening platform

Developing a high-throughput screening platform for antibody production using cell-free systems.
